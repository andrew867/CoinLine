# Support policy

## 0.1.0-alpha

This release is a **public alpha** of the **CoinLine Payphone Management Platform** — an OEM-grade fleet management stack for **Millennium payphones**. It is published so teams can **inspect architecture**, **run lab deployments**, and **plan field validation** — **not** as a commercially supported production product unless separately contracted.

| Expectation | Alpha stance |
|-------------|----------------|
| Availability / SLA | **None implied.** Builds may break between commits; fixes are **best-effort** when applicable. |
| Response time | **Not guaranteed.** Feedback may be incorporated asynchronously. |
| Production indemnity | **None.** Customers assume responsibility for deployment, compliance, and third-party certifications. |
| Training & onboarding | **Documentation and product package** unless separately arranged. |

## After alpha

Support tiers, SLAs, and escalation paths remain **organization-defined** for any future commercial program. This documentation does **not** imply 24/7 vendor support unless explicitly contracted.

## Security-sensitive issues

Treat suspected vulnerabilities as **confidential** until triaged. Use the **private security contact** your organization designates for this product (do **not** file exploitable details in public issues).

## Upgrades

Read **[Changelog](changelog.md)** and **[Known limitations](../reference/known-limitations.md)** before upgrading. Alpha to later versions may require **database migrations**, **configuration changes**, and **re-validation** on supported hardware.
